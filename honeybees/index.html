About Us

Welcome to Honeybees Preschool! We are a nurturing and inclusive preschool dedicated to providing a safe and loving environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive. At Honeybees, we believe in fostering a love for learning through play-based and age-appropriate activities that support children's social, emotional, and cognitive development.

Our passionate and experienced teachers are committed to creating a positive and engaging educational experience for your child. We emphasize building strong foundational skills, encouraging creativity, and promoting social interactions that help children build friendships and self-confidence.

What sets Honeybees apart:

  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • A curriculum designed to prepare children for a successful transition to kindergarten
  • A focus on social and emotional development through play and structured activities
  • A safe and secure environment for your child's well-being

We look forward to welcoming your child into our Honeybees family. If you have any questions or would like to schedule a visit, please don't hesitate to contact us. Together, we'll help your child embark on an exciting journey of learning and discovery.

Activities at Honeybees

Description of the Gandhi Jayanti Activity: Celebrating the birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi, the Father of the Nation, with various events and cultural programs. Participants pay tribute to his ideals of peace, non-violence, and civil rights.

Description of Karnataka Rajyotsava Activity: Celebrating Karnataka Rajyotsava, the state's formation day, with great enthusiasm and pride. This day marks the unification of all Kannada-speaking regions to form the state of Karnataka. The celebration includes cultural programs, parades, and showcasing the rich heritage and traditions of Karnataka.
